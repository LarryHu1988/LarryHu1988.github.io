滴水龙雨

生于滴水沿，现居滴水湖。
生肖属龙，生时逢雨。
半导体行业从业十二年，
从产品工程到工艺整合。
追梅西，忠巴萨，念科比。
看读库，读新诗，写小诗。
玩实况，打黑猴，码APP。

12 years in semiconductors,
from product engineering to process integration.
Messi follower, Barca culer, Kobe in memory.
A Duku reader and Chinese poetry enthusiast.
A gamer of eFootball and Black Myth.
An independent app developer.
